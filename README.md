HOW TO LAUNCH THE GAME:
1. You need to download the Visual Studio Code and Python3.13 to play with the game.
2. Click on the "New text file" button on the upper left side.
3. Click on the "Open folder" button on the left side (First, check if the smaller part of the second button in the upper right corner is lit white. If not click on it.).
4. Click on the third icon on the upper right side (This will open a small window at the bottom. If the smaller part already white, don't click on it.).
5. Click on the "TERMINAL" and press CTRL + S button.
6. Write to the terminal "pyton" right-click on the "Geometry_Dash" file and select "copy path".
7. Finally, click on the "TERMINAL" area and press CTRL + V and ENTER button.
8. Enjoy the game!
